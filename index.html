Kazuya Fujimoto

  • Assistant Professor, Department of Physics, Institute of Science Tokyo
  • Research interest
    Nonequilibrium statistical mechanics, Nonequilibrium quantum many-body systems, Ultracold atomic systems
  • Methods
    (Analytical method) Random matrix theory, Bethe ansatz, renormalization group, singular perturbation theory, weak wave turbulence theory, classical stochastic process methods, etc.
    (Numerical method) Time-evolving decimation method, density matrix renormalization group, exact diagonalization, pseudo-spectral method, etc.
